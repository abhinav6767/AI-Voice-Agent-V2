# Project Sunset - Real Estate Brochure

## Overview
Project Sunset is a premium luxury residential complex located in the heart of Mumbai (Andheri West). It offers world-class amenities, stunning sea views, and excellent connectivity.

## Key Features
- **Configurations:** 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK luxury apartments.
- **Starting Price:** ₹2.5 Cr for 2 BHK, ₹3.8 Cr for 3 BHK, ₹5.5 Cr for 4 BHK.
- **Amenities:** Olympic-size swimming pool, modern clubhouse, fully equipped gym, indoor games room, 24/7 security, lush green gardens, and dedicated children's play area.
- **Connectivity:** 5 mins from the metro station, 15 mins from the airport, close to top schools and hospitals.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
- **When is the possession date?** Possession will start by December 2026.
- **Are there any early bird discounts?** Yes, we are offering a 5% discount for bookings made before next month.
- **Is financing available?** Yes, we have tied up with HDFC, SBI, and ICICI for home loans up to 80%.
- **Can we visit the site?** Absolutely! Site visits are open every day from 9 AM to 6 PM.

## Agent Instructions
If the lead asks about the project, mention the premium location and the olympic-size swimming pool. If they ask about the price, check their budget first. Encourage them to book a site visit.
