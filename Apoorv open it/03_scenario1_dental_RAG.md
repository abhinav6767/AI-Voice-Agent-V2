# DELHI DENTAL CENTER - CLINICAL KNOWLEDGE BASE & OPERATIONS MANUAL

## 1. OFFICE LOCATION & LOGISTICS
- Clinic Name: Delhi Dental Center.
- Address: Block E, Connaught Place, New Delhi. Located near Rajiv Chowk Metro Station, Gate Number two (next to the main bank building).
- Parking: Valet parking is available at our front gate. Self-parking is available in the public Connaught Place Block E parking bays. Valet parking is free for patients during their consultation.
- Office Hours:
  * Monday to Saturday: ten AM to eight PM.
  * Sunday: Closed.

## 2. DENTAL PRACTITIONERS & SPECIALTIES
- Dr. Rohan Sharma: Lead General Dentist. Specializes in scaling, teeth polishing, fillings, crowns, and diagnostic assessments.
- Dr. Pooja Malhotra: Orthodontist and Certified Invisalign Aligner Specialist. Handles braces, bite corrections, and cosmetic alignments.

## 3. INSURANCE PANELS & BILLING SCHEMES
- Accepted Insurance: CGHS (Central Government Health Scheme) panel, Star Health Insurance, and Niva Bupa.
- Corporate Tie-Ups: We have cashless corporate panels for Tata Group and Reliance Industries employees.
- Non-Panel Policy: Patients using other insurers or corporate schemes must pay directly at the billing desk. We provide receipt dockets, stamped claim forms, and dental treatment summaries to support their direct reimbursement claims.
- Cashless Authorizations: Patients under CGHS or corporate panels must present their valid employee ID card or CGHS card at the check-in desk before their consultation starts.

## 5. APPOINTMENT & CHECK-IN POLICIES
- Late Arrivals: Patients coming more than twenty minutes late may have to wait for the next available slot or reschedule to prevent delay for other appointments.
- Cancellation and Rescheduling: Patients should inform us at least two hours in advance if they cannot make it.
- Fees: We do not charge cancellation or rescheduling fees, but appreciate timely updates.
- Age Limits: We consult children aged five and older. We do not perform pediatric procedures under general anesthesia at this clinic.

## 6. PRE-APPOINTMENT PATIENT GUIDELINES
- Documentation Check: All patients must bring a government photo ID (Aadhaar Card or PAN Card). Corporate and CGHS patients must bring their original physical cards.
- Dental Treatment Prep: If you have been prescribed pre-medications or antibiotic prophylaxis, ensure you take them exactly one hour before your appointment time.
